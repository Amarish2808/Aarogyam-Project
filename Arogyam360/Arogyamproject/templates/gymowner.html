Weight Training

#} {#

Weight training is a form of exercise that uses resistance—such as dumbbells, barbells, or machines—to build strength and muscle. It helps improve overall fitness by increasing muscle mass, boosting metabolism, and enhancing endurance. Regular weight training also supports better posture, joint health, and everyday functional movement.

Zumba

#} {#

Zumba is a high-energy fitness program that combines dance and aerobic movements with lively music. It focuses on fun, rhythm, and full-body movement to improve cardiovascular health and coordination. Suitable for all fitness levels, Zumba makes working out feel like a dance party.

Cardio

#} {#

Cardio is a type of exercise that raises your heart rate to improve heart and lung health. It includes activities like running, cycling, swimming, and aerobic workouts. Regular cardio boosts endurance, burns calories, and supports overall fitness and well-being.
